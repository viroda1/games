# 🌌 COSMIC SINGULARITY – Click to Grow the Abyss

**A cosmic clicker game where you evolve a singularity from a neutron star to a hyperquasar, collect Space Dust, buy upgrades, and unlock achievements.**

👉 **Play now:** [Live Demo](https://viroda1.github.io/games/) 

---

## 🚀 About the Game

You begin as a **Neutron Star** with a small gravitational pull. Each click feeds the singularity, increasing its mass. As mass grows, you evolve through celestial stages:

- **Neutron Star** → **Stellar Black Hole** → **Intermediate Void** → **Supermassive Abyss** → **White Hole** → **Hyperquasar**

Every click also generates **Space Dust** – a currency used to purchase cosmic upgrades that enhance your power, passive income, and dust gains.

---

## ✨ Features

- **6 unique cosmic stages** with distinct visuals and behaviors (inward/outward particle effects, gravitational lensing)
- **Upgrade system** with 4 upgradable paths:
  - ⚡ Gravitational Surge – increases mass per click
  - ✨ Dust Nebula – boosts dust per click
  - 🌀 Accretion Drift – passive dust generation
  - 💫 Singularity Echo – multiplies total mass gain
- **12+ achievements** to unlock, tracking milestones and dedication
- **Dynamic particle system** reacting to stage type (infalling accretion vs. outward white hole jets)
- **Responsive design** – works on desktop and mobile, with fullscreen support
- **Save-less but addictive** – each reset lets you start fresh, but the journey is the reward

---

## 🎮 How to Play

1. **Click** the central singularity to add mass and earn Space Dust.
2. Use **Space Dust** to buy upgrades in the panel below.
3. Progress through stages by reaching mass thresholds.
4. Unlock achievements by performing specific feats (e.g., 1000 total clicks, reaching the White Hole, etc.).
5. The game automatically generates a tiny amount of mass over time (cosmic drift) and passive dust from upgrades.
